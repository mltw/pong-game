Pong

How to play:

  1. Use 'W' key to move the paddle up, 'S' key to move it down.
  2. Defend the ball with your paddle.
  3. When the ball hits the wall of a player, the opponent scores a point.
  4. When a player reaches 7 points, he/she is the winner and the game ends.

    5. *After a player scores more than 3 points, the ball would move faster!

    Have Fun!
